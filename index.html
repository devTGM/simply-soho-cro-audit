01. Funnel Mechanics & AOV Drivers
High Impact: Critical Revenue Gap

Zero-Friction Transaction Infrastructure

The Bundle Gap: Currently, the site lacks a dynamic **"Frequently Bought Together" (FBT)** engine. For high-aesthetic homeware, purchase intent is often driven by "completing the set." Industry suppressive effect: 15-22% suppression of potential AOV.
Volume Pricing Absence: There is no technical evidence of quantity breaks (e.g., "Set of 4" discounts). This is a missed opportunity for bulk inventory movement.
Technical Execution: Deploy a meta-field based bundling logic. Pair every ceramic jar with matching plates/spoons. Use a "Smart Set Creator" widget below the primary fold that auto-updates price based on selection.
High Impact: Drop-off Accelerator

Checkout Velocity & Friction Log

Express Checkout Void: 70%+ of modern Indian e-commerce transactions originate from mobile wallets/UPI. Simply Soho lacks an above-the-fold **Express Pay Checkout** (Razorpay Magic, GPay).
Information Overload: The information stage requires 11 mandatory fields before final shipping confirmation. Expected Drop-off Rate: 35-40% at this stage due to form fatigue.
Technical Execution: Implement a single-click checkout solution (ShopFlo or Gokwik style) to pre-fill address details based on PIN. Place Express Pay button *above* the standard Checkout button on the Cart Drawer.
02. Aesthetic Precision & Brand Consistency
Medium Impact: Brand Equity Friction

Typographic Symmetry & Footer Integrity

Font Mismatch: While the Header utilizes a sophisticated serif, the Footer defaults to a generic system-weight sans-serif that lacks brand distinctness. This creates a "Template Trap" feeling.
Visual Jarring: The high-contrast dark green footer does not bridge with the airy, minimalist product photography above it.
Design Precision: Redesign the footer using **Gestalt principles of proximity**. Use a muted forest-green (#1f2e1f) and carry over the Playfair-Display font for footer headers. Add subtle micro-transitions to social icons to enhance the premium feel.
03. Search IQ & Information Architecture
Medium Impact: Logic Failure

Search UX & Zero-State Performance

Indexing Failure: Searching for core keyword "Handmade" yields only 2 results despite the entire brand ethos being handmade. This indicates a failure in internal **Tagging Strategy**.
Strategy: Overhaul SEO tagging for all SKUs. Implement a "Smart Search" that recommends trending categories when zero or low-results are found to prevent user bounce.

Structural Wins (Long Term)
